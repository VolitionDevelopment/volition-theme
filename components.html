Excerpt

♦

Excerpts are the main feature of the theme. They take up the whole width of the screen and are fully responsive to height. If you like to be fancy, you can put a triangle at the bottom.

Colors

♦

Excerpts can be white, grey, blue, green, red, black, dark grey, and clear.

Spacers

Spacers can be large, medium, small, or extra-small. They are simply blank space that reveal the background.

Cover

You can include an optional cover for the background image. The cover is in use now.

Simply remove the <div class="cover"></div> at the top of the page if you prefer not to use it.

Separator

♦

The theme comes with a fancy separator. You can insert it anywhere. If applied to a clear, dark grey, or black excerpt, it will appear white.
